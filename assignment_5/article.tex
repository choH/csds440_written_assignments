\section{Problem 19}

\begin{align*}
    b^T u &= u^T b \\
    &\leq u^T \cdot Ax \ \ \text{(Since $Ax \geq b$)}\\
    c^T X &= x^T c \\
    &\geq x^T A^T u \ \ \text{(Since $A^u \leq c$)}\\
    &\geq u^T Ax \\
    \Longrightarrow \ b^T u &\leq c^T X
\end{align*}

The statement is therefore proven.
